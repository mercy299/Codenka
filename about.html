About Us

Welcome, to Codenka

Africa's own Envato

At Codenka, we focus on giving individuals and buisnesses an opportunity to strengthen their bonds with customers through unique design templates.

We believe that art provides a means of expression which words fail to give and we trust that developers can leverage on our services to challenge themselves in the quality of work they deliver.

While serving the needs of developers and buisnesses in turn, we aim to provide a side hustle for our design contributors via our marketplace; thus providing an avenue for real lifework experiences and a fantasized get-paid-for-what-you-love-doing situation.
